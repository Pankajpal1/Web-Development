What is a framework? 
A library is something you can integrate in your code, and it gives you some functions and methods, and you decide
where to use them in your own code. eg. Axios, ASCII art library

With a framework, the framework controls how the code works, and we just follow the rules. Libraries do smaller things
frameworks have broader ambitions, like making web apps

Framework gives you an entire development environment
